ℹ️  About SJCEM Jobs Portal

Built for SJCEM Students

A dedicated portal designed to help engineering students find software and hardware fresher opportunities in Mumbai and Pune.

Our Purpose

Finding entry-level engineering jobs can be challenging due to cluttered platforms and hidden details. SJCEM Jobs Portal aggregates relevant listings to show location, salary, and requirements upfront.

How it works

The portal automatically collects fresh software and hardware engineering job postings for Mumbai and Pune. Stale listings older than 30 days are automatically removed to keep the job feed current.
