ℹ️  About Kizumi Jobs

Built for people who
do creative work

Kizumi Jobs strips out the noise of the modern job hunt and puts what matters — the role, the stack, and the salary — front and center.

The problem we solve

Modern job boards are bloated. You search, you filter, you scroll — only to click into a listing that hides the salary, buries the tech stack, and sends you off to yet another application form.

Kizumi Jobs aggregates real listings and surfaces the information you actually care about, before you ever click through.

How it works

Our backend syncs with the Adzuna API every morning at 5 AM, pulling the freshest creative and technical roles. Listings older than 30 days are automatically removed.

Everything is stored in Supabase and served through a Vercel edge function — so the dashboard loads fast, every time.

🎯

Focused

No sponsored posts, no recruiter spam. Just real roles from vetted sources.

⚡

Fast

Edge-cached API responses. The job feed loads in under 300ms on a good connection.
